Album Art

Back in high school, I used to make mixtape album art in Photoshop. So, when my friend asked me to develop a cover for her 2020 debut EP, I was eager to help—despite not having access to any design programs, at the time. I ended up layering several images in Microsoft Word, to produce the Five Petals of Venus cover for her! Since then, I’ve used Adobe Illustrator to create the rest of the art, below. I included Gustave Courbet’s The Desperate Man and Edvard Munch’s The Scream, on the cover of my foreign-language playlist, Let Me Out.
